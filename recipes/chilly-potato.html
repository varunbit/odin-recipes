Chilly Potato

Description

Chilli potato is an Indo-Chinese fusion recipe, popular in restaurant menu as well as street food. Crispy fried coated potatoes, tossed in a glossy honey-soy based sauce briefly, making it irresistible.

This flavoured dish makes a special starter on a weekend night.

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Honey
  • Garlic
  • Chilly
  • Corn flour
  • Green chilly
  • Tomato ketchup

Steps

  1. Peel potato and slice them to same thickness
  2. Rinse once and add potato to boiling water in a saucepan. Boil for 3 minutes. Drain from water completely.
  3. Take it in mixing bowl. Add 2 tablespoon of corn flour
  4. Heat a wok/ pan with oil. Following that, add finely chopped garlic and green chilli. Fry for 30 seconds and add sesame seeds. Roast until garlic starts to turn golden.
  5. Add red chilli powder, black pepper powder, soya sauce, chilli sauce and ketchup. Add a generous pinch of salt.
  6. Enjoy your dish
